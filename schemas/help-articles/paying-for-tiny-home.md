title: Payment Methods for Buying a Zen Tiny Home

Buying a Zen Tiny Home involves flexible payment methods including upfront deposits, progress billings for each construction phase, and final payments on delivery. Most clients use personal financing backed by banks or credit unions. Zen Tiny Homes staff guide buyers through all steps and paperwork, making the process transparent and manageable from start to finish.
